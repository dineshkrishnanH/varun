Our Mission

At Expense Manager, our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their finances with an easy-to-use and efficient platform. We aim to simplify the way users manage their income and expenses, helping them achieve their financial goals with better insights.

Whether it's tracking daily expenses, setting a monthly budget, or generating reports, Expense Manager ensures users have the tools they need to stay financially organized.

Technologies Used

  • Python Django: Handles backend logic, routing, and database interactions.
  • SQLite: Stores user data and expense records efficiently.
  • HTML5 & CSS3: Ensures a responsive, user-friendly interface.
  • Bootstrap 5: Provides styling and mobile responsiveness.
  • JavaScript (ES6): Adds interactivity to the platform.
